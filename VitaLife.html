Application Guidelines

1

Regular Inspection

Inspect wire ropes regularly to determine lubrication needs. Proper maintenance extends rope life by keeping it clean and preventing rust.

2

Frequency Over Quantity

Regular re-lubrication is more important than the quantity applied at one time. Maintain a consistent schedule for best results.

3

Field Considerations

Account for spillage and handling losses during field application. Use recommended quantities as a guide for your specific conditions.
