Standard Thimbles

Our G-414 Extra Heavy Wire Rope Thimbles provide superior protection for wire rope eyes in standard applications. Manufactured to exacting specifications for maximum strength and durability.

Ideal for: Rigging systems, lifting applications, and quick-connect scenarios
