Webbing Slings Overview

Our webbing slings are manufactured from 100% high-tenacity polyester yarn, offering superior strength and flexibility for a wide range of industrial lifting applications. Designed with safety as the top priority, these slings feature a 1:7 safety factor and are rigorously tested to exceed industry standards.

Simplex Slings

Single-layer construction preferred for broad width support. Ideal for straight pull applications with reinforced loops or metal fittings.

Duplex Slings

Double-layer construction for increased strength. Available with reinforced loops or metal end fittings for versatile use.

Triplex Slings

Triple-layer heavy-duty slings with 1:7 safety factor. Engineered for demanding industrial lifting requirements.

Quadraplex Slings

Quadruple-layer maximum strength slings for the most challenging lifting applications with exceptional durability.
