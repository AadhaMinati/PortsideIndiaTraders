Lowering Belts Overview

Our lowering belts are engineered for heavy-duty pipe lowering operations, manufactured from duplex polyester webbing joined with heavy-duty steel terminals. Designed for superior safety, durability, and ease of use in demanding industrial environments.

High Load Capacity

Engineered for lowering heavy pipes with capacities up to 50,000 kg

Enhanced Safety

Low elasticity and UV-resistant materials ensure reliable performance

Quick Replacement

Advanced terminal design allows for simple on-site belt replacement

Long Service Life

Economical solution with extended durability in demanding conditions
