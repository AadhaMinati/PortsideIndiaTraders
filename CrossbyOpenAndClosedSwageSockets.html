Swage Sockets Overview

Our swage sockets are manufactured from high-grade forged steel, offering superior strength and reliability for critical lifting applications. Designed with safety as the top priority, these sockets undergo rigorous testing to exceed industry standards and ensure maximum performance.

Closed Swage Sockets

Designed for permanent wire rope terminations with a seamless, high-strength connection. Ideal for applications requiring maximum strength and reliability.

Open Swage Sockets

Feature an open design for easy rope insertion, providing a secure termination point while allowing for adjustments or replacements.

Quality Assurance

Each socket undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets or exceeds international standards for strength, durability, and safety.

Technical Specifications

All our swage sockets are manufactured to exacting standards with forged steel construction. Maximum Proof Load shall not exceed 50% of XXIP rope catalog breaking strength.
