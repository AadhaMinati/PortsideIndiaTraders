Endless Round Slings - KR Series

Key Features

  • Lightweight compared to working load ratio
  • Polyester material - no rust or corrosion
  • No danger of hand injury from cut wires
  • Does not spark - safe for explosive environments
  • Low elongation (3%) - absorbs load while lifting
  • Flattens and adjusts to contours for firm grip

Construction

  • Bearing elements are endlessly laid with high-tenacity polyester fiber strands
  • Protective sleeve made of special heavy-duty polyester fabric
  • SWL increases with number of fiber strands

Specifications

  • Factor of safety for webbing: 1:7
  • Colors are indicative and may vary based on material availability
  • Minimum breaking load is 7 times SWL

Safety Information

  • Always inspect slings before use for cuts, abrasions, or damage
  • Do not exceed the working load limit for the specific lift configuration
  • Protect slings from sharp edges and abrasive surfaces
  • Store slings in a clean, dry environment away from direct sunlight
  • Regularly inspect for wear and tear - replace damaged slings immediately
