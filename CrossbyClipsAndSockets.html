Installation & Application

Selection & Preparation

Choose the correct clip or socket size based on your wire rope diameter. Ensure all components are clean, undamaged, and free from corrosion. For clips, determine the required number based on rope diameter and application.

Proper Installation

For clips: Position the U-bolt over the dead end of the rope with the saddle on the live end. Tighten nuts evenly to the recommended torque. For sockets: Insert the rope through the socket body, place the wedge, and apply tension to secure.

Inspection & Maintenance

Regularly inspect for signs of wear, deformation, or corrosion. Check for proper seating of all components. Maintain correct torque on nuts. Replace any components showing excessive wear or damage. Follow manufacturer's maintenance schedule.
